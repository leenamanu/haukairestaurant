About Us



Haukai Restaurant specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.

The Restaurant was started by a couple who wanted the tourist to experience Maori Kai.

Our food menu are designed to bring authentic flavours of Maori kai.



We take booking for table reservation over the phone or email , bookings can be done online as well through Reservation

Culture



Māori culture and values shape the everyday life of people in New Zealand. Māori culture (Māori: Māoritanga) is the customs, cultural practices, and beliefs of the indigenous Māori people of New Zealand. It originated from, and is still part of, Eastern Polynesian culture.


Māori were traditionally hunters and gatherers, harvesting food from the forest, sea, stream and gardens. Māori have been cooking with ingredients from New Zealand's native forests for more than a thousand years.

New Zealand's seafood is world famous. Try our rich crayfish, succulent king salmon and tasty green-lipped mussels.
